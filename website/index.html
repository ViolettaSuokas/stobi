FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Stobi different?

Stobi is a creative hobby, not a game. You paint real stones, hide them on real walks, and every stone you release keeps its own story — you'll always know where it's been and which smile it's in today. Find a stone? It's yours to keep, display at home, or hide again somewhere new.

Is Stobi really free?

Yes — painting, hiding, finding, chatting, following every stone's journey, and customizing your mascot is completely free. Premium (€3.99/mo) is optional and unlocks exclusive cosmetics, streak freezes, and priority support.

How does Stobi protect my location?

We store approximate locations only — never your exact coordinates. Stones are placed on a fuzzy grid (~100m) so no one can track you home. Your real-time location never leaves your device.

What age is Stobi for?

Stobi is 13+. We verify age on sign-up and apply stricter moderation to keep the community safe. Content is curated to be family-friendly: no harassment, no explicit content, no personal contact details.

How do I earn diamonds 💎?

Every find earns 1–3 💎, every hide earns 5 💎, and when a stranger finds your stone you get 3 💎 more. Daily challenges (find 5 stones) unlock a 24-hour Premium taste. Spend diamonds on mascot cosmetics and map boosts.

Can I use Stobi outside Finland?

The map is currently focused on Finland — that's where we're launching first. Global chat works everywhere, so you can share stone art and ideas with painters worldwide. More countries coming soon.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete account. Your data is removed within 30 days per GDPR. You can also email hello@stobi.app and we'll handle it for you.
