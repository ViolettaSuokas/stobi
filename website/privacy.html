Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026

Stobi ("we", "our", "the app") is a painted-stone geocaching app built by Violetta Suokas, based in Helsinki, Finland. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it. We aim to be plain-spoken, not lawyerly.

Data we collect

  • Account info — if you register: email address, username, optional profile photo, optional character name, birth year (for age verification).
  • Location — approximate GPS coordinates when you hide or find a stone, so we can verify you are near it. We do not track you continuously.
  • Photos — photos you upload of stones you paint or find. EXIF (including GPS) is stripped before upload.
  • Activity — stones you hide, stones you find, chat messages, achievements, and diamond balance.
  • Analytics — anonymous events (e.g., "opened map", "completed onboarding") to help us improve the app.
  • Crash reports — technical data (device type, OS version, stack traces) when the app crashes, via Sentry.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not share your precise GPS coordinates with other users. Hidden stones show with randomized offsets.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not allow users under 13 (COPPA / GDPR).

Where your data lives

Data is stored on Supabase servers in the EU (Frankfurt). We use RevenueCat for subscription management and Sentry for crash reporting.

Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access — request a copy of your data.
  • Correction — fix anything wrong.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all data from Settings → Delete account. Takes effect within 30 days.
  • Portability — export your data in machine-readable form.
  • Opt-out — disable analytics in Settings.

Children

Stobi is for ages 13 and up. We ask for birth year at signup. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, email us at hello@stobi.app and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will post the new version here and update the "last updated" date. Major changes will be announced in the app.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@stobi.app.
