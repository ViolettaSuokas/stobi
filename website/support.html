Quick answers

How do I find a stone?

Open the Map tab. Painted stones near you appear as pins. Tap a pin, walk within 30 meters of the stone, and tap "Found". You'll earn 💎 diamonds.

How do I hide a stone?

Open the + tab in the bottom bar. Snap a photo of your painted stone, write a description, drop a pin, and save. You earn diamonds when others find it.

I can't log in / forgot my password

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password". We'll send you an email link. If you don't see it, check spam.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings → Delete account. All your data will be removed within 30 days.

How do I cancel Premium?

Premium is managed by App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Open your device's subscription settings and cancel. Access continues until the end of the billing period.

Referral code doesn't work

Codes can only be applied once per user, during registration or within your first session. You can't apply your own code.

Still stuck?

Email us at hello@stobi.app. We usually reply within 1-2 days. Include:

  • Your username or email
  • What went wrong
  • Screenshot if possible
  • Device (iPhone / Android)
